# FitForge Product Overview

FitForge is a modern health and fitness application that combines personalized workout plans, nutrition tracking, and AI-powered coaching to help users achieve their fitness goals.

## Core Features

- **AI Coach**: Personalized fitness guidance and recommendations
- **Workout Management**: Custom workout plans, exercise tracking, and session management
- **Nutrition Tracking**: Meal planning, calorie counting, and dietary goal management
- **Progress Monitoring**: Weight tracking, workout statistics, and performance analytics
- **Subscription System**: Free and premium tiers with Stripe integration
- **User Profiles**: Comprehensive user data including goals, preferences, and progress history

## User Types

- **Regular Users**: Access to basic features, workout tracking, and limited AI coaching
- **Premium Users**: Full access to AI coach, advanced analytics, and premium content
- **Admins**: Administrative dashboard for user management and system oversight

## Key Business Logic

- Users complete onboarding to set fitness goals and preferences
- AI coach provides personalized recommendations based on user data
- Progress tracking includes weight history, workout completion, and streak tracking
- Premium features are gated behind subscription validation
- Daily tracking includes water intake, sleep, steps, and calories
